### 5.1 Advanced Multimedia Generation (52 hours)

- **Content Enhancement**: AI-powered cross-referencing, fact-checking, and quality scoring
- **Multi-format Export**: Support for HTML, PDF, PowerPoint, and interactive formats

### 5.2 Real-Time Collaborative Features (60 hours)

- **Version Control**: Advanced change tracking and collaborative editing

### 5.3 Intelligent Automation (66 hours)
**Automate knowledge management with AI-driven insights**

- **Smart Recommendations**: Personalized content and learning path suggestions
- **Workflow Automation**: Automated content processing and quality assurance
- **Predictive Analytics**: Usage patterns, performance forecasting, and trend analysis
- **Machine Learning**: Continuous improvement through user behavior analysis

### 5.4 Advanced Analytics and Insights (52 hours)
**Comprehensive business intelligence and performance monitoring**

- **Executive Dashboards**: High-level analytics with custom visualization capabilities
- **Advanced Reporting**: Automated report generation with scheduled delivery
- **Business Intelligence**: ROI analysis, knowledge asset valuation, and compliance reporting
- **Real-time Metrics**: Performance monitoring and system health analytics

### 5.5 Performance Optimization (52 hours)
